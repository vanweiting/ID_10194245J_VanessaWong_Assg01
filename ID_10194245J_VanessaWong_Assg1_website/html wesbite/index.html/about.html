About the band and how their name came about

Given(ギヴン) is an amateur rock band. The members create time to gather at a studio to play and create music and songs and they occasionally play shows for many to here and also to promote themselves. Most amateur rock bands choose their band names randomly and it is no different for Given. While deciding on their band name, one of the members, Mafuyu, told the others that he started playing the guitar as it was given to him after an emotional event. That was how they decided on their band name, "Given".
